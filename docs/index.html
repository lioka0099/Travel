Top Attractions in Argentina

Iguazú Falls, Misiones

Iguazú Falls is a breathtaking spectacle of 275 individual waterfalls spanning almost 2 miles. A fun fact about Iguazú Falls is that it's surrounded by lush tropical rainforest, making it a haven for exotic wildlife and plants.

Recoleta Cemetery, Buenos Aires

Recoleta Cemetery is a grand necropolis that's often referred to as the 'city of the dead'. A fun fact about Recoleta Cemetery is that it's the final resting place of Eva Perón, the former First Lady of Argentina.

Perito Moreno Glacier, Santa Cruz

Perito Moreno Glacier is a majestic glacier located in Los Glaciares National Park. A fun fact about Perito Moreno Glacier is that it's one of the few glaciers in the world that's still growing, due to its unique location and climate.
