Top Attractions in Panama

Panama Canal, Panama City: The Panama Canal is one of the most complex engineering projects ever undertaken, connecting the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.

Casco Viejo, Panama City: This historic district is known for its charming cobblestone streets, colonial architecture, and vibrant nightlife.

Boquete, Chiriqui: Boquete is a charming town in the mountains, known for its coffee plantations, hiking trails, and adventure activities like rafting and zip-lining.
