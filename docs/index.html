Top Attractions in Spain

Alhambra, Granada: The Alhambbra is a palace and fortress complex that showcases Moorish architecture, featuring intricate Islamic calligraphy and geometric patterns.

Sagrada Familia, Barcelona: Antoni Gaudí's iconic cathedral is known for its unique and intricate design, taking over 130 years to complete.

Museo del Prado, Madrid: As one of the world's greatest art museums, the Prado features an extensive collection of European art, including works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.
