Top Attractions in israel

The Western Wall, Jerusalem: A sacred site in Judaism, the Western Wall is the last remaining wall of the Second Temple. Interestingly, the Wall is not a single wall but two separate walls: the larger, more visible section and a smaller section hidden from view.

Masada, Masada National Park: Perched atop a rock plateau, Masada is an ancient fortification that has witnessed numerous historical events. A fun fact is that Masada was the site of a famous siege by the Roman Empire in 73 CE.

Tel Aviv Beach, Tel Aviv: Known for its vibrant nightlife and beautiful beaches, Tel Aviv Beach is a must-visit. A fun fact is that Tel Aviv has the highest number of cafes per capita in the world.
