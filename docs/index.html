Argentina Travel Guide

Top Attractions

Teatro Colón, Buenos Aires

Teatro Colón is one of the world's most famous opera houses, known for its stunning architecture and exceptional acoustics. Did you know it's considered one of the top five opera houses in the world?

Iguazú Falls, Puerto Iguazú

Iguazú Falls is a breathtaking spectacle of 275 individual waterfalls spanning nearly 2 miles. A fun fact is that the falls are so massive they create their own microclimate, often shrouded in mist.

Perito Moreno Glacier, El Chaltén

Perito Moreno Glacier is a majestic glacier located in Los Glaciares National Park. An interesting fact is that it's one of the few glaciers still in advance, meaning it's still growing.
