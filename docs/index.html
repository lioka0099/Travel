Top Attractions in Argentina

Teatro Colón, Buenos Aires

Teatro Colón is one of the world's most renowned opera houses, known for its exceptional acoustics and ornate architecture. Fun fact: It took over 28 years to complete its construction, finally opening in 1908.

Iguazú Falls, Misiones

Iguazú Falls is a breathtaking spectacle of nature, consisting of over 275 individual waterfalls. Fun fact: The falls are surrounded by lush tropical rainforest, making it a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Perito Moreno Glacier, Santa Cruz

Perito Moreno Glacier is a majestic glacier located in Los Glaciares National Park. Fun fact: It's one of the few glaciers in the world that is still growing, and it's a popular spot for glacier trekking.
