Top Attractions in Spain

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona

La Sagrada Familia is a famous cathedral designed by Antoni Gaudí, known for its unique and intricate architecture. Fun fact: It has been under construction since 1882 and is expected to be completed by 2026.

Alhambra, Granada

The Alhambra is a medieval Islamic palace and fortress complex that showcases Moorish architecture. Fun fact: It is one of the most visited monuments in Spain, attracting millions of visitors each year.

Museo del Prado, Madrid

The Museo del Prado is one of the world's greatest art museums, housing an extensive collection of European art. Fun fact: It contains over 8,000 works of art, including masterpieces by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.
