Top Attractions

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona - La Sagrada Familia is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Antoni Gaudí's most famous works, known for its intricate architecture and long construction period that has spanned over a century.

Alhambra, Granada - The Alhambbra is a palace and fortress complex that showcases Moorish architecture, featuring intricate calligraphy, geometric patterns, and serene gardens.

Museo del Prado, Madrid - The Museo del Prado is one of the world's greatest art museums, housing an extensive collection of European art, including works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.
