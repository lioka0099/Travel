Top Attractions in Germany

Neuschwanstein Castle, Füssen: This fairytale-like castle was the inspiration for Disney's Sleeping Beauty Castle and was built in the 19th century for King Ludwig II.

Berlin Cathedral, Berlin: The Berlin Cathedral is a stunning example of late 19th-century architecture and offers panoramic views of the city from its dome.

Marienplatz, Munich: The heart of Munich's old town, Marienplatz is known for its iconic Glockenspiel, which chimes and reenacts stories twice daily.
