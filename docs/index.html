Top Attractions in Argentina

Teatro Colón, Buenos Aires: This stunning opera house is considered one of the world's best, known for its exceptional acoustics and ornate decor. Fun fact: It took 28 years to complete its construction.

Iguazú Falls, Misiones Province: A breathtaking spectacle of 275 individual waterfalls, Iguazú Falls is a natural wonder that attracts visitors from around the globe. Fun fact: The falls are surrounded by lush subtropical rainforest.

Perito Moreno Glacier, Santa Cruz Province: A majestic glacier in Los Glaciares National Park, Perito Moreno is known for its dramatic calving events. Fun fact: It's one of the few glaciers in the world that is still growing.
