Top Attractions in israel

Masada, Southern District - Masada is an ancient fortress located on a plateau in the Judean Desert, near the Dead Sea. Fun fact: Masada was the site of a famous siege by the Roman Empire in 73 CE.

The Western Wall, Jerusalem - The Western Wall is a sacred site in Judaism, located in the Old City of Jerusalem. Fun fact: The Western Wall is the last remaining wall of the Second Temple, destroyed by the Romans in 70 CE.

Old Jaffa, Tel Aviv - Old Jaffa is a historic neighborhood in Tel Aviv, known for its narrow streets, ancient architecture, and artistic community. Fun fact: Old Jaffa has been a source of inspiration for many artists and writers, including S.Y. Agnon, Israel's first Nobel laureate.
