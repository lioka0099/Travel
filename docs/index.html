Top Attractions in Spain

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona - La Sagrada Familia is a famous cathedral designed by Antoni Gaudí, known for its unique and intricate architecture. Fun fact: La Sagrada Familia has been under construction since 1882 and is expected to be completed by 2026.

Alhambra, Granada - The Alhambbra is a medieval Islamic palace and fortress complex, showcasing Moorish architecture. Fun fact: The Alhambra is considered one of the greatest examples of Islamic art and architecture in the world.

Museo del Prado, Madrid - The Museo del Prado is one of the world's greatest art museums, housing an extensive collection of European art. Fun fact: The Prado Museum has over 8,000 works of art on display, including masterpieces by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.
