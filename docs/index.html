Top Attractions in Italy

Colosseum, Rome: This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and hosted gladiator battles and public events.

Grand Canal, Venice: The main waterway through Venice is known for its stunning architecture and romantic gondola rides.

Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore, Florence: This terracotta-tiled cathedral complex is a masterpiece of Renaissance architecture and features a dome designed by Filippo Brunelleschi.
