Top Attractions in Montenegro

Kotor Old Town, Kotor: Kotor's historic old town is a walled city with a rich history, featuring narrow cobblestone streets and ancient architecture. One fun fact about Kotor Old Town is that it is surrounded by the world's southernmost fjord, making it a unique and breathtaking destination.

Cetinje, Cetinje: Cetinje is a historic town that was once the capital of Montenegro. It is known for its museums, galleries, and historic buildings. A fun fact about Cetinje is that it is home to the Biljarda Museum, which was the residence of Prince-Bishop Petar II Petrović-Njegoš.

Lovćen National Park, Njeguši: Lovćen National Park is a beautiful park featuring stunning mountain scenery, hiking trails, and historic landmarks. A fun fact about Lovćen is that it is home to the mausoleum of Petar II Petrović-Njegoš, a famous Montenegrin poet and philosopher.
