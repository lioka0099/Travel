Top Attractions in Spain

Top Attractions

Alhambra, Granada - The Alhambra is a palace and fortress complex that contains some of the most remarkable Islamic architecture in the world, with intricate stone carvings and serene gardens.

Casa Batlló, Barcelona - Casa Batlló is a famous house designed by Antoni Gaudí, known for its colorful facade, irregular windows, and unique architectural style that has become a symbol of Barcelona.

Santiago de Compostela Cathedral, Santiago de Compostela - The Santiago de Compostela Cathedral is a magnificent Gothic cathedral that is said to contain the remains of Saint James the Greater, making it a major pilgrimage site.
