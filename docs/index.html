Top Attractions in Japan

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto - Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates, Fushimi Inari is a breathtaking Shinto shrine. Visitors can hike the torii gate trail for spectacular views.

Tokyo Tower, Tokyo - At 332.9 meters tall, Tokyo Tower is an iconic communications tower and one of Japan's most recognizable landmarks. It offers panoramic views of the city from its observation decks.

Mount Aso, Kumamoto - One of the world's largest active volcanoes, Mount Aso offers stunning landscapes and outdoor activities like hiking and horseback riding.
