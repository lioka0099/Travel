Discover Japan

Top Attractions

Tokyo Skytree, Tokyo: At 634 meters tall, Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world and offers breathtaking views of the city.

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto: Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates, Fushimi Inari Shrine is a must-visit for any traveler to Japan.

Miyajima Island, Hatsukaichi: Known for its stunning scenery and historic landmarks, Miyajima Island is a popular day-trip destination from Hiroshima.
