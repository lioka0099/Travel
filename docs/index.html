Top Attractions in Israel

Masada, Masada National Park - Located in the Judean Desert, Masada is an ancient fortification on a rocky plateau. Fun fact: Masada was the site of a famous siege by the Romans in 73 CE.

The Dead Sea, Tamar Regional Council - A saltwater lake bordering Jordan, Israel, and Palestine. Fun fact: The Dead Sea is known for its buoyant waters due to its high salinity.

Yad Vashem, Jerusalem - Israel's official memorial to the victims of the Holocaust. Fun fact: Yad Vashem is a complex that includes a museum, a research center, and a tree-lined Avenue of the Righteous Among the Nations.
