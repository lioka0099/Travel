Welcome to Japan

Top Attractions

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto - Known for its thousands of vermilion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain.

Shibuya Crossing, Tokyo - One of the busiest intersections in the world, known for its buskers, street food, and fashionable crowds.

Kiyomizu-dera Temple, Kyoto - A UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its wooden stage protruding over the valley below, offering a panoramic view of Kyoto.
