Top Attractions

Iguazú Falls, Misiones Province

Iguazú Falls is a breathtaking spectacle of 275 individual waterfalls, spanning almost 2 miles. A fun fact about Iguazú Falls is that it's surrounded by lush tropical rainforest, making it a unique and unforgettable experience.

Recoleta Cemetery, Buenos Aires

Recoleta Cemetery is a grandiose cemetery that's often referred to as a "city of the dead." With elaborate mausoleums and statues, it's a fascinating and somewhat eerie attraction. A fun fact is that it contains the grave of Eva Perón, also known as Evita.

Perito Moreno Glacier, Los Glaciares National Park, Santa Cruz Province

Perito Moreno Glacier is one of the few glaciers in the world that's still growing. Located in Los Glaciares National Park, it's a stunning sight to behold, with its towering ice walls and turquoise glacial lake. A fun fact is that it's one of the most accessible glaciers in Patagonia.
