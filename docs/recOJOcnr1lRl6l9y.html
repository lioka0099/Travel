Travel Guide to India

Must-see Attractions

  • Taj Mahal, Agra – Made entirely of white marble.
  • Gateway of India, Mumbai – A monument erected to commemorate the visit of King George V.
  • Golden Temple, Amritsar – A gurudwara with a temple made of gold.
