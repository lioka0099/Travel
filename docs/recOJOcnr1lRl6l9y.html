Top Attractions in Japan

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto - Famous for its thousands of vermillion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain.

Meiji Shrine, Tokyo - Dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken, it's a serene escape from the bustling city.

Kiyomizu-dera Temple, Kyoto - A UNESCO World Heritage site, it's famous for its wooden stage protruding over the valley below, offering a panoramic view of Kyoto.
