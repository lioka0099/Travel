Top Attractions

Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum made of white marble, built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is considered one of the most beautiful examples of Mughal architecture, a blend of Indian, Persian, and Islamic styles.

Red Fort, Delhi

The Red Fort is a historic fort in Delhi, India, that served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is considered one of the most iconic landmarks in India.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is a gurudwara (Sikh temple) in Amritsar, Punjab. It is one of the most sacred sites in Sikhism and is known for its golden dome and beautiful architecture.
