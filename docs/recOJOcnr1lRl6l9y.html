Travel Guide: India

Attractions

  • Taj Mahal, Agra – Made entirely out of white marble.
  • Gateway of India, Mumbai – A monument built to commemorate the visit of King George V.
  • Kanyakumari Temple, Kanyakumari – Located at the southernmost tip of India.
