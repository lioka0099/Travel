Must-visit Attractions

Taj Mahal, Agra: The Taj Mahal changes color depending on the time of day and the weather, appearing pinkish in the morning, white during the day, and golden at night.

Varanasi Ghats, Varanasi: The Varanasi Ghats are a series of steep stairs leading down to the Ganges River, with over 80 ghats along the riverfront, each with its own significance.

Red Fort, Delhi: The Red Fort was built by Shah Jahan in 1648 and served as the main imperial palace of the Mughal Empire, showcasing a blend of Indian, Persian, and Islamic architectural styles.
