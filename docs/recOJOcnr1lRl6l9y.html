Argentina Travel Guide

Top Attractions in Argentina

1. Teatro Colón, Buenos Aires

Teatro Colón is a world-renowned opera house in Buenos Aires, known for its stunning architecture and exceptional acoustics. One fun fact about Teatro Colón is that it was considered one of the top five opera houses in the world when it reopened in 2010 after a major renovation.

2. Iguazú Falls, Misiones

Iguazú Falls is a breathtaking waterfall system on the border of Argentina and Brazil. A fun fact about Iguazú Falls is that it's made up of over 275 individual waterfalls spanning almost 2 miles, making it one of the largest waterfall systems in the world.

3. Perito Moreno Glacier, Santa Cruz

Perito Moreno Glacier is a stunning glacier in Los Glaciares National Park, Patagonia. A fun fact about Perito Moreno Glacier is that it's one of the few glaciers in the world that's still growing, due to its unique location and the cold climate.
