Popular Attractions

Grand Palace, Bangkok

The Grand Palace is a must-visit attraction in Bangkok, featuring a stunning array of temples, halls, and buildings. The palace has been the official residence of the king and queen of Thailand since 1782. Visitors can explore the complex and see the iconic Emerald Buddha.

Phang Nga Bay, Phuket

Phang Nga Bay is a beautiful bay located in Phuket, known for its towering limestone cliffs and turquoise waters. Visitors can take a boat tour to explore the bay and see the famous James Bond Island. The bay is also home to a variety of marine life, including monkeys and eagles.

Doi Suthep, Chiang Mai

Doi Suthep is a mountain located just outside of Chiang Mai, offering stunning views of the city and surrounding countryside. Visitors can take a tram or hike to the top of the mountain, where they can see the beautiful Wat Phra That Doi Suthep temple and enjoy the cool mountain air.
