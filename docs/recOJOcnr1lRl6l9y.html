Top Attractions in Argentina

Teatro Colón, Buenos Aires

Teatro Colón is a world-renowned opera house in Buenos Aires, known for its stunning architecture and exceptional acoustics. Fun fact: It's considered one of the top five opera houses in the world.

Iguazú Falls, Puerto Iguazú

Iguazú Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder on the border of Argentina and Brazil. Fun fact: The falls consist of over 275 individual cascades spanning nearly 2 miles.

Perito Moreno Glacier, El Calafate

Perito Moreno Glacier is a majestic glacier in Patagonia, known for its vibrant blue color and dramatic calving events. Fun fact: It's one of the few glaciers in the world that's still growing.
