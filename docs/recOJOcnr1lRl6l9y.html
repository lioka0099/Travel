Top Attractions

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Fushimi Inari Shrine is located in Kyoto, Japan. A fun fact about this shrine is that it's famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain.

Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower is located in Tokyo, Japan. This communications tower stands at 333 meters tall and was the tallest tower in the world when completed in 1958.

Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is an active volcano located on Honshu Island, Japan. It's the country's tallest peak and a sacred symbol, often depicted in Japanese art.
