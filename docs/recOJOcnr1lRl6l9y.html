Popular Attractions

The Grand Palace, Bangkok

A must-visit attraction in Bangkok, the Grand Palace is a complex of buildings that has been the official residence of the king and queen of Thailand since 1782. It is known for its stunning architecture and historical significance. Visitors can explore the palace grounds and see the famous Emerald Buddha.

Phuket Patong Beach, Phuket

Patong Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Phuket, known for its vibrant nightlife, water sports, and lively atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy a range of activities, from swimming and sunbathing to jet-skiing and parasailing. The beach is lined with restaurants, bars, and shops, making it a great spot to relax and have fun.

Ayutthaya Historical Park, Ayutthaya

Ayutthaya Historical Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that showcases the ancient city of Ayutthaya, which was the capital of Thailand from 1350 to 1767. The park features numerous ancient temples and ruins, including Wat Phra Si Sanphet and Wat Mahathat. Visitors can explore the park by bike or on foot, taking in the history and culture of the area.
