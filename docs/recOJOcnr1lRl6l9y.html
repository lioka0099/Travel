Top Attractions in Argentina

Iguazú Falls, Misiones

Iguazú Falls is a breathtaking spectacle of 275 individual waterfalls, spanning almost 2 miles. A fun fact about Iguazú Falls is that it's surrounded by lush tropical rainforest, making it a unique ecosystem.

Teatro Colón, Buenos Aires

Teatro Colón is one of the world's most renowned opera houses, known for its stunning architecture and exceptional acoustics. An interesting fact about Teatro Colón is that it was considered one of the top five opera houses in the world upon its opening in 1908.

Perito Moreno Glacier, Santa Cruz

Perito Moreno Glacier is a majestic glacier located in Los Glaciares National Park, Patagonia. A fascinating fact about Perito Moreno Glacier is that it's one of the few glaciers in the world that's still growing, due to its unique location and climate conditions.
