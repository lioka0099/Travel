Top Attractions in Argentina

Teatro Colon, Buenos Aires

Teatro Colon is one of the world's most famous opera houses, known for its stunning architecture and exceptional acoustics. Did you know that it was designed by Italian architects and took over 20 years to complete?

Iguazu Falls, Puerto Iguazu

Iguazu Falls is a breathtaking spectacle of 275 individual waterfalls spanning over 2.7 kilometers. Fun fact: The falls are surrounded by lush tropical rainforest, making it a haven for wildlife and nature lovers.

Perito Moreno Glacier, El Calafate

Perito Moreno Glacier is a majestic glacier located in Los Glaciares National Park. Interestingly, it's one of the few glaciers in the world that's still growing, and visitors can witness its dramatic calving events.
