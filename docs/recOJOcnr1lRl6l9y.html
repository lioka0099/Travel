Must-visit Attractions

Tokyo Skytree, located in Tokyo, is the tallest tower in the world, standing at 634 meters. Fun fact: It is designed to withstand earthquakes and typhoons.

Fushimi Inari Shrine, situated in Kyoto, is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates. Fun fact: The shrine is dedicated to Inari, the Shinto god of rice.

Miyajima Island, near Hiroshima, is known for its scenic beauty and historic landmarks. Fun fact: The island is famous for its Itsukushima Shrine, which appears to be floating during high tide.
