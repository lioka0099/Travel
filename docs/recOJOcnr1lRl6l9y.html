Thailand

Attractions

The Grand Palace, located in Bangkok, is one of Thailand's most iconic landmarks and was the official residence of the king and queen of Thailand. The palace is known for its intricate architecture and beautiful gardens. It is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Bangkok.

Phi Phi Islands, situated in the Andaman Sea, are known for their stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters. The islands are popular for snorkeling and diving due to their rich marine life. The Phi Phi Islands were made famous by the movie "The Beach."

Wat Phra That Doi Suthep, located in Chiang Mai, is a sacred Buddhist temple perched on a mountain. The temple is famous for its golden chedi and offers panoramic views of Chiang Mai. According to legend, the temple was built after a monk had a vision that the temple should be built on this site.
