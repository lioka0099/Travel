Top Attractions in Montenegro

Old Town Kotor, Kotor: Kotor's Old Town is a walled medieval city with narrow streets and well-preserved architecture, surrounded by imposing fortifications.

Budva Old Town, Budva: Budva's Old Town is known for its ancient citadel, beaches, and vibrant nightlife, making it a popular destination.

Ostrog Monastery, Nikšić: Ostrog Monastery is a remarkable example of Montenegrin spirituality, perched on a cliff, attracting pilgrims and hikers alike.
