Top Attractions in Spain

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona

La Sagrada Familia is a famous cathedral designed by Antoni Gaudí. Fun fact: It's been under construction since 1882 and is expected to be completed in 2026.

Museo del Prado, Madrid

The Museo del Prado is one of the world's greatest art museums. Fun fact: It houses an extensive collection of European art, including works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.

Alhambra, Granada

The Alhambra is a palace and fortress complex that showcases Moorish architecture. Fun fact: It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Spain's most visited attractions.
