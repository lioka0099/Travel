Top Attractions in Spain

Museo del Prado, Madrid: The Museo del Prado is one of the world's greatest art museums, featuring works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco. Fun fact: The museum's collection includes over 8,000 paintings.

La Sagrada Família, Barcelona: La Sagrada Família is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most famous landmarks in Barcelona. Fun fact: The construction of La Sagrada Família began in 1882 and is still ongoing.

Cathedral of Seville, Seville: The Cathedral of Seville is the largest Gothic church in the world and a must-visit attraction in Seville. Fun fact: The cathedral's Giralda Tower offers stunning views of the city.
