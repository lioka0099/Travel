Top Attractions in Spain

Top Attractions

Alhambra, Granada - The Alhambra is a palace and fortress complex that was originally constructed as a small fortress in AD 889. It's known for its Islamic architecture and beautiful gardens.

Sagrada Familia, Barcelona - The Sagrada Familia is a famous cathedral designed by Antoni Gaudí. It's known for its unique and intricate architecture, and is still under construction.

Museo del Prado, Madrid - The Museo del Prado is one of the world's greatest art museums, featuring a vast collection of European art from the 12th to the 19th centuries.
