Top Attractions in Spain

Top Attractions

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona - Did you know that La Sagrada Familia is still under construction and is expected to be completed by 2026, marking the centenary of Antoni Gaudí's death?

Alhambra, Granada - The Alhambra is a palace and fortress complex that showcases Moorish architecture, and its name comes from the Arabic 'al-Hamra', meaning 'the red one', likely due to the reddish color of its walls.

Museo del Prado, Madrid - The Museo del Prado is home to an extensive collection of European art, including works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco, making it one of the world's greatest museums.
