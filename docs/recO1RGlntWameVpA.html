Top Attractions in Spain

Must-Visit Attractions

Alhambra, Granada

The Alhambra is a breathtaking palace and fortress complex that showcases Moorish architecture. A fun fact about Alhambra is that its name translates to "the red one" due to the reddish color of its walls.

Museo del Prado, Madrid

The Museo del Prado is one of the world's greatest art museums, housing an extensive collection of European art. Interestingly, the Prado has over 8,000 works on display, including masterpieces by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.

Park Güell, Barcelona

Park Güell is a public park featuring stunning architecture by Antoni Gaudí. Did you know that the park was originally intended to be a residential development, but it was converted into a public park due to lack of interest?
