Top Attractions in Spain

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona: Fun fact - La Sagrada Familia is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most famous landmarks in Spain, known for its unique and intricate architecture. Construction began in 1883 and is still ongoing.

Museo del Prado, Madrid: Fun fact - The Museo del Prado is one of the world's greatest art museums, housing a vast collection of European art, including works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.

Alhambra, Granada: Fun fact - The Alhambra is a medieval Islamic palace that showcases Moorish architecture, featuring intricate gardens, fountains, and stunning views of the city.
