Top Attractions in Spain

Top Attractions

Alhambra, Granada

The Alhambra is a palace and fortress complex that was originally constructed as a small fortress in AD 889. One fun fact about the Alhambra is that it is considered one of the most well-preserved Islamic palaces in the world.

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona

La Sagrada Familia is a large unfinished Catholic church designed by Antoni Gaudí. A fun fact about La Sagrada Familia is that it is expected to be completed in 2026, 100 years after Gaudí's death.

Museo del Prado, Madrid

The Museo del Prado is a museum of art located in Madrid, Spain. One fun fact about the Museo del Prado is that it houses an extensive collection of European art, including works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.
