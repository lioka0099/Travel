Top Attractions

Alhambra, Granada - A fun fact about Alhambra is that it is a palace and fortress complex that was originally constructed as a small fortress in AD 889.

Sagrada Familia, Barcelona - Sagrada Familia is a famous cathedral designed by Antoni Gaudí, and it is known for its unique and intricate Gothic and Art Nouveau style.

Royal Palace of Madrid, Madrid - The Royal Palace of Madrid is the official residence of the Spanish royal family, although it is only used for state ceremonies.
