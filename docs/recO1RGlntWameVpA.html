Top Attractions in Spain

Top Attractions

Alhambra, Granada: The Alhambra is a palace and fortress complex that was originally constructed as a small fortress in AD 889. One fun fact about the Alhambra is that it is considered one of the most important Islamic architecture monuments in the world.

Museo del Prado, Madrid: The Museo del Prado is the main Spanish national art museum, located in Madrid. A fun fact about the Museo del Prado is that it houses an extensive collection of works by Goya, including his famous 'The Third of May 1808'.

La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona: La Sagrada Familia is a large unfinished Catholic church designed by Antoni Gaudí. A fun fact about La Sagrada Familia is that it is expected to be completed by 2026, marking the centenary of Gaudí's death.
