Top Attractions in Belize

The Great Blue Hole, Belize City

The Great Blue Hole is a stunning underwater sinkhole that's over 300 meters in diameter. A fun fact about this natural wonder is that it's so big that it can be seen from space!

Caracol, San Ignacio

Caracol is an ancient Mayan temple that's deep in the jungle. Interestingly, it was once a major city, and its remains still hold many secrets.

Lamanai, Orange Walk Town

Lamanai is another significant Mayan archaeological site, known for its well-preserved structures. A fun fact is that its name means "submerged crocodile," reflecting its history.
