Top Attractions

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto

Fushimi Inari Shrine is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain. It's a breathtaking sight and a great spot for hiking.

Tokyo Skytree, Tokyo

Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world, standing at 634 meters. It offers panoramic views of the city from its observation decks.

Osaka Castle, Osaka

Osaka Castle is a majestic castle that played a crucial role in the unification of Japan. It now serves as a museum and a popular tourist destination.
