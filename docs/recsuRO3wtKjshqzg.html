Top Attractions in Israel

Masada, Masada National Park - Located on a mountain plateau, Masada is an ancient fortification that was once the site of a famous siege by the Romans. Fun fact: Masada is known for its stunning views of the Dead Sea.

The Western Wall, Jerusalem - The Western Wall, also known as the Wailing Wall, is a sacred site in Judaism and a remnant of the Second Temple. Fun fact: Visitors can insert prayer notes into the cracks of the wall.

Tel Aviv Promenade, Tel Aviv - The Tel Aviv Promenade, also known as the Tayelet, is a scenic pedestrian path along the Mediterranean coastline. Fun fact: The promenade is lined with restaurants, cafes, and street performers.
