Top Attractions in israel

Must-see Attractions

Masada, Masada National Park - Located near the Dead Sea, Masada is an ancient fortification on a rocky plateau. Fun fact: Masada was the site of a famous siege by the Romans in 73 AD.

The Western Wall, Jerusalem - A sacred site in Judaism, the Western Wall is a remnant of the Second Temple. Fun fact: The Western Wall is also known as the Wailing Wall due to the traditional Jewish practice of wailing or lamenting there.

Tel Aviv Promenade, Tel Aviv - A popular destination for locals and tourists, the Tel Aviv Promenade is a scenic walkway along the Mediterranean coast. Fun fact: The promenade is lined with Bauhaus architecture, a style that is characteristic of Tel Aviv.
