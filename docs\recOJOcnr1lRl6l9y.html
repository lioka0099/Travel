Top Attractions

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto

Fushimi Inari Shrine is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates forming a tunnel up the mountain. It's a breathtaking sight and a great spot for hiking. The shrine is dedicated to Inari, the Shinto god of rice.

Tokyo Tower, Tokyo

Tokyo Tower is a communications tower built in 1958, inspired by the Eiffel Tower. It stands at 332.9 meters tall and offers panoramic views of the city from its observation decks. At night, it's illuminated, making it a beautiful sight.

Mount Fuji, Yamanashi

Mount Fuji is Japan's highest mountain and an iconic symbol of the country. It's a popular destination for hiking and climbing, with several trails leading to the summit. The mountain is considered sacred and is surrounded by five lakes.
