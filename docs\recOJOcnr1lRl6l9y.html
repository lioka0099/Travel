Top Attractions

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto - Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates, Fushimi Inari Shrine is a breathtaking sight. The shrine is dedicated to Inari, the Shinto god of rice. Visitors can hike the famous orange gate trail.

Kiyomizu-dera Temple, Kyoto - A UNESCO World Heritage site, this temple is renowned for its wooden stage protruding over the valley below. Visitors can enjoy the panoramic views of Kyoto. The temple is also famous for its cherry blossom viewing in spring.

Tokyo SkyTree, Tokyo - At 634 meters tall, Tokyo SkyTree is the tallest tower in the world. It offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks. Visitors can also enjoy shopping and dining at the tower's base.
