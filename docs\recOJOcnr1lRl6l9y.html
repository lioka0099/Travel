Attractions

The Grand Palace in Bangkok is a must-visit attraction, known for its stunning architecture and historical significance. It's the official residence of the king and queen of Thailand since 1782. Visitors can see the Phra Thinang Chakri Group, which houses the offices of the king.

Phuket Big Buddha is located on top of the Nakkerd Hills between Chalong and Kata beaches. This 45-meter-tall marble Buddha is an iconic symbol of Phuket. Visitors can enjoy stunning views of the island from the site.

Wat Phra That Doi Suthep is a Buddhist temple in Chiang Mai, perched on a mountain peak. The temple is known for its golden chedi and the views of Chiang Mai city below. Visitors can see the naga (serpent) staircase leading to the temple.
