Top Attractions

Fushimi Inari Shrine, located in Kyoto, is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain. It's a breathtaking sight to walk through the gates and explore the shrine. The shrine is dedicated to Inari, the Shinto god of rice.

Tokyo Tower, situated in Tokyo, is a communications tower that stands 333 meters tall, offering stunning views of the city from its observation decks. It was built in 1958 and was inspired by the Eiffel Tower. Visitors can enjoy shopping and dining at the tower's facilities.

Mount Fuji, near Tokyo, is Japan's highest peak and a iconic symbol of the country. It's a popular destination for hiking and trekking, with several routes to the summit. The mountain is considered sacred and is surrounded by five lakes.
