Attractions

The Grand Palace, located in Bangkok, is one of Thailand's most iconic landmarks and a must-visit attraction. It served as the official residence of the king and queen of Thailand since 1782. The palace is known for its elaborate architecture and intricate details.

Wat Phra Kaew, also in Bangkok, is a temple complex within the Grand Palace grounds. It's famous for housing the revered Emerald Buddha. The temple is rich in history and showcases stunning Thai architecture.

Phuket Big Buddha, situated on top of the Nakkerd Hills between Chalong and Kata, is a 45-meter tall marble Buddha statue. It is one of the most revered Buddha images in Thailand, symbolizing peace and stability.
