Attractions

The Grand Palace, Bangkok - The Grand Palace is one of Bangkok's most famous landmarks and a must-visit attraction. It has been the official residence of the king and queen of Thailand since 1782. The palace is known for its stunning architecture and intricate details.

Wat Phra Kaew, Bangkok - Wat Phra Kaew is a temple complex located within the Grand Palace grounds. It is home to the famous Emerald Buddha, a highly revered Buddha image in Thailand. Visitors can explore the temple's stunning architecture and learn about its rich history.

Phuket Big Buddha, Phuket - The Phuket Big Buddha is a 45-meter-tall marble Buddha statue located on top of a hill in Phuket. It is one of the largest Buddha statues in Thailand and offers stunning views of the surrounding islands. Visitors can also explore the nearby temples and learn about the local culture.
