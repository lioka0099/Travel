Top Attractions

Fushimi Inari Shrine, located in Kyoto, is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates forming a tunnel up the mountain. It's a breathtaking sight, especially during sunrise or sunset. Visitors can hike up the mountain through the gates.

Tokyo Skytree, in Tokyo, is the tallest tower in the world, offering panoramic views of the city from its observation decks. It's a must-visit for its height and the excellent views it provides. The tower is surrounded by a beautiful park.

Miyajima Island, near Hiroshima, is known for its scenic beauty and historic landmarks, including the famous Itsukushima Shrine, which appears to be floating during high tide. The island is also home to friendly deer and beautiful beaches. Visitors can enjoy hiking and scenic boat rides.
