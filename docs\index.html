The Grand Palace

Bangkok, Thailand. The Grand Palace is one of Bangkok's most famous landmarks and a must-visit attraction, known for its stunning architecture and historical significance.

Phuket Big Buddha

Phuket, Thailand. The Phuket Big Buddha is a 45-meter-tall statue of Buddha on top of a hill in Phuket, offering panoramic views of the island.

Wat Rong Khun

Chiang Rai, Thailand. Wat Rong Khun, also known as the White Temple, is a contemporary, unconventional Buddhist temple known for its unique architecture and intricate details.
